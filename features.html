Budgets

Budgets that
actually adapt.

Set spending limits per wallet, optionally scoped to a category. Budgets auto-advance each period so you never have to reset them. The rollover feature carries unspent amounts forward — capped sensibly by default.

↩ How Rollover Works
1.Budget limit: 200,000
2.You spent: 140,000 — 60,000 unspent
3.Next period limit: 260,000 (capped at 200,000 by default)
4.Card shows: ↩ carried from last period
  • ✓Alert threshold (default 80%) with push notification hooks
  • ✓Custom rollover cap to prevent snowballing
  • ✓Full period history tracked across all recurring cycles

Analytics

Understand where
your money goes.

Beautiful charts built with Chart.js give you a clear picture of your finances. All charts respect your active date range and wallet filter, and fully support dark mode.

  • ✓Pie / Donut chart — category breakdown by expense or income
  • ✓Line chart — income vs expenses, Daily / Weekly / Monthly
  • ✓Savings rate ring — % of income saved with full breakdown
  • ✓Top categories with ranked coloured progress bars
Cloud Backup

Your data,

  • ✓One-tap restore — even from a fresh install
  • ✓Backup data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • ✓Sign in with email & password or Google

Notifications

Stay ahead
of your spending.

My Budget sends timely push notifications so you never overspend or forget a recurring payment — without being intrusive.

  • ✓All notifications can be customised or turned off per type
